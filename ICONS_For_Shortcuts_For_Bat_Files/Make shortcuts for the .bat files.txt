Make shortcuts for these bat files:
* "Run_Rando_Prompto.bat"
* "Run_Phrase_List_Manager.bat"

Then open the shortcut files properties and change the icon to the provided icons.
Just navigate to the folder where to unzipped the app files and find 
this folder, and select the icon you want to use. Or pick your own icons.
Then you can place the shortcuts wherever you want on you computer (like the desktop).
